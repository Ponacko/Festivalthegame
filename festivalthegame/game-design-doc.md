# Festival: The Game

## High Concept
A top-down 2D festival-life simulation where players attend a real or procedurally generated multi-day music festival, balancing personal needs while maximizing enjoyment by seeing favorite artists.

## Core Pillars
- Personalized festival experience based on music taste.
- Strategic time and resource management.
- Social interactions with friends and other attendees.
- Authentic festival atmosphere and logistics.

## Player Setup
- Import favorite artists from music services or enter them manually.
- Recommend a historical or current multi-day festival matching the player’s tastes, or generate a custom festival.
- Import or generate lineups, stage schedules, stage locations, and festival maps.
- Ask whether the player is attending with friends and the size of the group.

## Gameplay Loop
1. Choose a campsite location.
2. Plan which concerts and activities to attend.
3. Travel between stages, food stalls, toilets, and camp.
4. Manage needs and budget.
5. Sleep or socialize at night.
6. Repeat for each festival day and maximize total enjoyment score.

## Player Needs
- Hunger
- Thirst
- Energy
- Comfort
- Social
- Fun
- Bladder

If a need reaches zero, the player may suffer penalties such as passing out and missing festival time.

## Enjoyment System
- Favorite artists grant the highest enjoyment.
- Similar genres provide moderate enjoyment.
- Closer positions to the stage increase enjoyment.
- Mosh pits increase fun but consume energy and carry an injury risk.
- Bonus points are awarded if imported favorite songs appear in actual setlists.

## Friends
- Friends have randomized schedules.
- Spending time together increases social and fun.
- Camping near friends provides convenience benefits.

## Resources
- Finite budget for food and drinks.
- Food items vary in nourishment and food-poisoning risk.
- Drinks may boost fun and socialization while affecting bladder and energy.

## Camping and Sleep
- Campsite location affects noise and rest quality.
- Nighttime choices trade energy for fun and social interaction.

## Festival Simulation
- Concert attendance is derived from artist popularity metrics.
- Crowds influence stage access and movement.

## Map and Navigation
The player can open a map displaying:
- Campsite
- Stages
- Food stalls
- Toilets
- Other points of interest

## Technical Requirements
- Top-down 2D presentation.
- Tap-to-move controls.
- Data import interfaces for music services.
- Fallback procedural generation for unavailable data.

## Success Condition
Achieve the highest possible festival enjoyment score before the festival ends.
